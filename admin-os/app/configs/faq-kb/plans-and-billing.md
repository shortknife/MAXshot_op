# Plans and Billing FAQ

## What does the Pro plan include?
The Pro plan includes priority support, advanced analytics, team workspaces, and higher monthly usage limits. It is intended for teams that need deeper reporting and faster support response times.

## How can customers upload invoices?
Open the billing area, select **Invoices**, and use the upload action to attach a PDF or image file. After upload, the document enters a review step before it becomes part of the billing record.

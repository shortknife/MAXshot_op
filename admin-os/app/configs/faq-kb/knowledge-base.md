# Knowledge Base FAQ

## How do I upload a knowledge base document?
Open the knowledge base area, choose **Add document**, upload a Markdown, PDF, or text file, and wait for validation to finish. Files that fail validation stay in review until corrected.

## What happens when an answer is low confidence?
Low-confidence answers should not be presented as final grounded answers. They are returned with a bounded fallback response and can be marked for manual review.

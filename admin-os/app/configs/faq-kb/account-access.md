# Account Access FAQ

## How do I reset my password?
Use the **Forgot password** link on the sign-in page. Enter your account email, submit the request, and use the password reset link sent to your inbox. If the email does not arrive within a few minutes, check spam first and then contact support.

## How do I update my login email?
Open account settings, go to **Profile**, change the email field, and confirm the change from the verification email.

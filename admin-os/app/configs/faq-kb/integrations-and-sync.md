# Integrations and Sync FAQ

## Can I sync the knowledge base with another system?
The current slice supports controlled uploads and review-ready sources. Automatic external sync is not available in the bounded FAQ plane unless a dedicated integration source is approved.

## How are imported documents validated?
Documents are checked for readability, section structure, and readiness before they can be treated as grounded FAQ material. Sources that fail QC remain in review instead of becoming active immediately.
